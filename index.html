History of Transformation

From teaching foundational computing to pioneering AI-driven research, this timeline traces a 22-year arc of deliberate, institution-shaping transformation — teaching, grant-winning, and building frameworks others follow.

Joined as faculty in Computer Science, teaching foundational courses and simultaneously co-ordinating the Centre for Information Technology — building the campus management system from the ground up. Began integrating interdisciplinary perspectives long before it was mainstream.

Enrolled in Ph.D. at Karunya University focused on automatic cancer detection in mammograms. Won Best Paper Award at ICAC-09 and again in 2010 at ICCCI'10. Published first Scopus-indexed research. Presented at 6th Pan-Asian Service-Learning Conference in Hong Kong. The research focus that would define the next decade was firmly set.
